Solar Year & Lunar Month Definitions

Astronomical Solar Year

A Tropical year (365.242199) is a measure of the Sun's passage from one mean vernal equinox to the next.

A Sidereal year (365.256366) is a measure of Earth's complete orbit around the Sun relative to fixed stars.

An Anomalistic year (365.259636) is a measure of earth's passage from one perihelion to another.

Time Period (Daśa) Year Definition
Astronomical Lunar Month

A Tropical month (27.321582) is a measure of the Moon's passage from one mean vernal equinox to the next.

A Sidereal month (27.321662) is a measure of Moon's complete orbit around the Earth relative to fixed stars.

An Anomalistic month (27.321582) is a measure of Moon's passage from one perihelion to another.

A Synodic month (29.530588) is a measure of Moon's passage from one New Moon to another.
