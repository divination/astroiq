About us

AstroIQ is a result of the co-operation between Andri Lindbergs and Neil Gardner, starting in November 2016.

The aim of this venture is to provide a complete app for astrologers, with all the most important astrological traditions and methods.

This venture is also intended to bring astrologers together and provide means for them to share their knowledge and experience with others.

Finally this venture is intended to provide a bridge between astrologers and their clients.
